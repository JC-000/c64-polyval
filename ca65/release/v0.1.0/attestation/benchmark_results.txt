Cycle counts (VICE-measured, min over many iterations; LONG profile unless
noted). Min over median is reported because the VICE binary monitor
occasionally injects stalls that inflate medians while the lower bound is
stable against the original text-monitor numbers to within 1 cycle.

  polyval_double:                85 cy min
  polyval_shift_left_4:         411 cy min
  polyval_xor_table_entry:      175 cy min
  polyval_precompute_table:  255259 cy min   (110 cy FASTER than ACME)
  polyval_update (multi-block): per-block deltas within VICE measurement
                                noise across N = 1, 4, 16, 64, 256 blocks.

Summary: cycle-parity with ACME on the polyval_multiply hot path, a small
(+110 cy) improvement on polyval_precompute_table, and no regressions on
any benchmarked routine.
